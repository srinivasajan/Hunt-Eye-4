What HuntEye Is

Camera

The app needs a video source. That can be a fake practice feed, your webcam, or a simulator camera.

Target

The green box shows what HuntEye is watching. In practice mode, it is simulated so you can learn safely.

Safety

Safety rules limit speed, altitude, and emergency states before movement commands are accepted.

AirSim

AirSim is a drone simulator. If it is closed, simulator mode cannot connect. Practice mode still works.

System Check

This checks whether the project is healthy. It does not require a real drone.
